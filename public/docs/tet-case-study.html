How TET migrated from Teachable to Shopify with a full custom build.
TET — Trusted English Trainers — had outgrown Teachable. We migrated the full platform to Shopify: custom Figma-to-Shopify theme, blog, product pages, and mobile-optimized components.

The Client
Trusted English Trainers

TET — Trusted English Trainers — is an English language learning platform built on years of teaching expertise. Their courses are structured for working professionals and students who need practical, results-driven English instruction, not classroom theory.

By the time they came to Scalo, TET had outgrown Teachable. The platform was limiting their ability to control the brand experience, customize the purchase journey, and manage their catalog the way a growing course business needs to.

The Challenge
Teachable Was a Ceiling, Not a Foundation

Teachable works until it doesn't. For TET, the limitations were stacking up: no control over the storefront design, no ability to build a branded blog experience, constrained product page layouts, and mobile UX that didn't match the quality of the course content itself.

The migration wasn't just a technical move — it was a brand upgrade. Moving to Shopify meant rebuilding everything: the storefront, the course product pages, the blog, and all the mobile-facing components that learners interact with most.

"The goal wasn't to replicate Teachable on Shopify. It was to build the platform TET should have had from the start — full brand control, custom purchase flow, and a mobile experience that matches the content quality."

Area Teachable (Before) Shopify (After)
Storefront Platform-controlled, minimal customization Full custom Figma-to-Shopify theme
Blog Not available / limited Custom blog with brand-matched design
Product pages Teachable template, no layout control Custom course PDPs built to convert
Mobile experience Responsive but not optimized Mobile-first components built to spec
Brand control Constrained to platform defaults Full Figma design system implemented
What We Built
A Custom Shopify Platform, Built from Figma

The engagement started in Figma — every section designed to spec before a line of Liquid was written. The handoff was clean: design system established, component library documented, then translated into a fully custom Shopify theme.

01 · Theme
Full Custom Shopify Build
New theme built from scratch in Liquid — not a Shopify template customization. Every section designed in Figma first, then implemented to exact spec with a full schema for content management.
02 · Blog
Branded Editorial Experience
Custom blog design and template system — article pages, archive, category navigation. Built to match the brand and load fast on mobile, where most readers land.
03 · Course Pages
Product Pages Built for Courses
Shopify product pages adapted for the course purchase flow — curriculum overviews, instructor context, testimonials, and enrollment CTAs structured to convert course buyers, not product shoppers.
04 · Mobile
Mobile-First Component Design
Every component built mobile-first. TET's learners access content on mobile at high rates — the navigation, hero, course cards, and checkout flow were all optimized for sub-400px viewports before scaling up.
05 · Design System
Figma → Shopify, No Guesswork
The Figma design system served as the single source of truth. Tokens defined in Figma mapped directly to CSS custom properties in the theme — typography, color, spacing, and component behavior all consistent.
06 · Migration
Content & Catalog Moved Over
Course catalog migrated from Teachable to Shopify products — titles, descriptions, pricing, and structure preserved. Content audit completed before cutover to ensure nothing was lost in translation.
The Takeaway
Platform Choice Is a Brand Decision

Course creators often stay on platforms like Teachable long after they've outgrown them because the migration feels risky. TET made the move at the right time — before the limitations were costing them measurably.

The result is a platform that TET owns fully: the design, the purchase flow, the content structure. No platform tax on the brand experience. Full control over what learners see before they buy.

"Moving from Teachable to Shopify isn't just a technical migration — it's a statement about where the brand is going. TET needed a platform that could grow with them. That's what we built."

— Scalo · TET Engagement
