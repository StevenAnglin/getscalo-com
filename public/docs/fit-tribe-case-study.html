The Brand
A high-volume activewear brand

Fit Tribe is an E£60M+ Egyptian activewear brand (~$1.18M USD) that built real traffic momentum on TikTok. By Q4 2025, they had 352K sessions and E£8.67M in revenue (~$170K USD) — but TikTok traffic, which made up 40% of sessions, was converting at 0.1%. Their site wasn't built to absorb cold social traffic at scale.

Navigation was undifferentiated. Search was buried. Product pages didn't do the persuasion work needed for visitors with zero prior brand familiarity. TikTok traffic landed on collection pages with no clear path to the right product. Mobile visitors were dropping off before they ever made a decision.

Activewear Egyptian market E£60M+ brand
The Challenge
Scaling Traffic Lowered CVR by 52% — TikTok Accelerated the Drop.

General scaling across all channels dropped CVR ~16% from Jan 2025 to Jan 2026, independent of TikTok. On top of that, TikTok — 40% of traffic — had fallen from ~2% CVR to 0.1%. Together, blended CVR dropped from ~1.46% to ~0.70%, a ~52% collapse. [(1.46 − 0.70) / 1.46 ≈ 52%]

The site had no mechanism to recover those visitors. Navigation was undifferentiated. Search was buried. Product pages didn't do persuasion work. TikTok traffic landed on collection pages with no clear path to the right product. Mobile visitors were dropping off before they ever made a decision.

TikTok accounted for 40% of traffic and had a 0.1% conversion rate. That was the drag. Fix the site for cold traffic, and blended CVR goes up even as TikTok scales.

The Approach
We optimized product discovery and navigation.

30-day sprint. We audited the full funnel and found the highest-leverage gap: visitors couldn't find exactly what they were looking for. Navigation, search, and collections weren't built for people arriving with intent but no brand familiarity. We rebuilt product discovery for all traffic — the fix served cold visitors and returning customers equally.

Search rebuilt as a discovery channel
Search was practically invisible — 30 total item searches at 0.016% of sessions. We elevated it in the UI, rebuilt the search modal with optimized product cards, and improved search suggestions. Search usage grew from 30 to 5,158 searches. 3% of users now use search — and they convert at 10.8%.
0.016% → 3% search usage 30 → 5,158 item searches 10.8% search CVR Est. E£900K+/month from search channel
Color options shown as real product images
Abstract color dots were replaced with real product images per variant. We linked products that shared the same silhouette but different colorways, then surfaced them as image swatches across PDPs and collection cards. Shoppers see exactly what they're buying before they click.
Image swatches across all PDPs Linked variant groups Faster product decisions Removes the biggest pre-click drop-off point
Cart rebuilt to convert, not just collect
ATC abandonment was at 75.88%. We added a tiered rewards progress bar to create purchase momentum, rebuilt line items with visual color previews and price highlights, added cart add-on options, and handled pre-order/standard split checkout. ATC abandonment fell to 59%.
75.88% → 59% ATC abandonment Tiered rewards bar Cart add-ons ~70% more purchases per ATC event
Mobile navigation rebuilt for intent-driven traffic
The previous nav had no clear path for visitors arriving with intent but zero brand familiarity. We rebuilt it mobile-first: search in the nav, mega menu cards with product imagery, a support menu, returns portal, and re-routed all destinations to the highest-value pages. Bounce rate dropped — though TikTok's outsized volume made clean attribution difficult.
Mobile-first rebuild Mega menu + search Support + returns in nav Enabled 21% traffic scaling without CVR collapse
PDP rebuilt to close cold traffic
PDP abandonment was 95.9% in December 2025. We rebuilt the page entirely — image overlays for zoom and care instructions, a pill-design media gallery, product badges with a badge drawer, highlighted payment options, image swatches, optimized reviews, cleaner variant picker, inventory status, improved size guide, accordion descriptions, and a product recommendations + recently viewed section. PDP abandonment is now 67%.
95.9% → 67% PDP abandonment Full page rebuild Recommendations + recently viewed 8× more PDP visitors completing purchase
Collections rebuilt as a real discovery layer
Large 100+ product collections had no real discovery infrastructure. We added subcollections, infinite scroll, spec-based filtering, image scrolling on product cards, a multi-step quick add with an info drawer before cart commitment, in-stock-only defaults, product tags, a features tab for badge control, and an optimized sorting menu. Collection abandonment dropped from 98.45% to 70% — and will fall further as ad traffic stabilizes.
98.45% → 70% collection abandonment Spec-based filtering Quick add with info drawer ~19× more collection visitors converting

The Results
Traffic Up 21%. Revenue Up 16%. CVR Held.

The comparison is Q4 2025 vs. Q1 2026. Sessions grew 21% — driven by continued traffic scaling across channels, TikTok the largest driver. Blended CVR rose from 1.16% to 1.24% despite TikTok accounting for 40% of traffic at 0.1% CVR. General scaling was compressing CVR on its own — TikTok compounded that. Product discovery and navigation improvements pulled it back up for all traffic.

After TikTok budget was re-allocated, CVR rose to 1.86%. The optimization had compounded — the site was converting better across all channels, not just compensating for TikTok's drag.
